Job Description

Verde Living Estates is seeking a senior Landscaping Architect to lead the design and execution of a multi-acre luxury residential estate in Beverly Hills, California. This is a high-value contract engagement focused on architectural landscaping, terrain engineering, and premium outdoor living design. This is a premium commission-based contract focused on sustainable luxury and structural integrity.

The ideal candidate will collaborate closely with architects, structural engineers, and estate managers to deliver a seamless integration of landscape, hardscape, and environmental systems in compliance with California zoning and sustainability guidelines.

Role Responsibilities

  • Design large-scale residential landscape master plans including grading, drainage, and irrigation systems.
  • Coordinate with structural engineers and local permitting authorities for code-compliant execution.
  • Oversee subcontractors for masonry, stonework, and outdoor architectural features.
  • Manage on-site project timelines and ensure adherence to premium quality standards.
  • Maintain full project documentation through the Prime Local professional portal.

Ideal Candidate

  • 8+ years of experience in luxury residential landscaping or exterior architectural design.
  • Demonstrated expertise in California-native planting and sustainable water systems.
  • Strong portfolio of high-end residential estates or private developments.
  • Excellent project management and client-facing communication skills.
  • Proven experience delivering multi-phase projects in regulated environments.
