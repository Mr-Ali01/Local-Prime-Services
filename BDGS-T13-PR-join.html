Common Onboarding Questions

When do I start receiving leads?

Once your manual verification audit is complete (usually 24-48 hours), your listing goes live immediately.

Is verification mandatory?

Yes. To maintain our premium ecosystem, every professional must pass our documentation and stand-of-work audit.

Can I pause my listing?

If you are overbooked, you can toggle your visibility to "Busy" to pause inquiries without cancelling your subscription.

How do payments work?

Customers pay you directly. We do not take a percentage of your job revenue. Your subscription covers your listing and lead gateway.
