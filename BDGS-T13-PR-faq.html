Home Repairs Support

Are plumbers and electricians on the platform licensed?
Yes. Every Home Repair professional must submit current state or local trade licenses for manual verification before their profile is activated.
Is emergency same-day service available?
Many pros list "24/7" or "Same-Day" on their profiles. For urgent issues, we recommend calling these providers directly for the fastest response.
Do pros provide their own materials?
Pros bring standard tools. For large components (water heaters, panels), the pro will usually provide a quote for materials or you can supply them.
Are structural repairs covered?
Yes, we host verified contractors for foundation, roofing, and wall repairs. These pros are audited for specific heavy-duty certifications.
Do I need a permit for home repairs?
Permit requirements vary by city. Your verified pro can advise you on local building codes and handle permit filings if needed.
Can I see photos of previous work?
Every pro profile includes a "Portfolio" section with high-resolution photos of their past structural and repair projects for your review.
Is there a warranty on repair work?
Most Prime Local pros offer a 30 to 90-day workmanship warranty. Specific terms are listed under the "Policies" tab of each pro.
Do you have masonry specialists for marble?
Yes, we have verified masonry experts who specialize specifically in premium stone polishing and Italian marble tiling.
Are pros insured for property damage?
Manual verification includes checking for active general liability insurance to ensure your home is protected during repairs.
How are estimates calculated?
Pros usually provide estimates based on labor hours and material costs. Some offer flat rates for standard jobs like switch replacements.
Can I hire for small handyperson tasks?
Yes. Many pros list "Handyperson" services for minor fixes like mounting TVs or fixing cabinet hinges with minimum call-out fees.
What if I have an electrical emergency?
Filter results by "Emergency" or "Available Now" to find electricians who specialize in rapid fault finding and power restoration.

Cleaning Support

What is included in a "Deep Clean"?
A deep clean covers baseboards, inside appliances, scrubbing grout, and detailed dusting. Check individual checklists on pro profiles.
Do I need to be home for the session?
Recommended for the first visit. Many users provide lockbox codes or digital access for recurring weekly cleaning sessions.
Are cleaning supplies provided?
Standard pros bring their own chemicals and equipment. You can request eco-friendly or specific vacuum types via the message system.
How long does a "Move-Out" clean take?
Deep move-out cleans typically take 4 to 8 hours depending on square footage to ensure a deposit-ready home.
Are your cleaning pros bonded?
Yes. We prioritize cleaning agencies that are fully bonded and insured for residential entry and key management.
Can I schedule a recurring weekly clean?
Yes. You can manage recurring bookings through your user dashboard and maintain the same cleaner for consistency.
What is "Upholstery Care"?
This involves steam-cleaning or dry-cleaning sofa fabrics, mattresses, and car interiors using specialized industrial equipment.
Do you offer post-construction cleaning?
Yes. Specialized crews handle paint debris, fine dust, and construction waste after home remodels.
Are the cleaning products safe for pets?
Many of our top-rated pros use non-toxic, eco-friendly products. You can filter pros by the "Eco-Friendly" tag.
Is tipping expected for cleaners?
Tipping is entirely optional but appreciated for exceptional service. You can tip directly after the session.
How is key security managed?
Pros follow a strict protocol for key handling. We recommend using digital lock codes which can be reset for your safety.
What if something is broken during cleaning?
Report it within 24 hours. Verified cleaning agencies carry liability insurance to cover property accidental damage.

Appliance Support

Is there a fee just for the professional to visit?
Most pros charge a "Diagnostic Fee" to identify the issue. This is often waived if you hire them to perform the repair.
How are replacement parts sourced?
Technicians typically source OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) parts from local hubs to ensure the fastest turnaround time.
Do pros fix premium smart brands?
Yes. We have specialists certified for brands like Sub-Zero, Viking, and smart LG or Samsung appliances.
Is repair better than replacement?
Technicians provide a "Repair vs Replace" assessment during diagnostics to help you decide based on unit age and efficiency.
Can AC gas be refilled same-day?
Yes. Authorized AC pros carry standard refrigerant gases and can perform refills and leak detection on-site.
Do you fix commercial restaurant equipment?
Yes. We have a specific sub-category for "Commercial Appliance" verified for industrial ovens and walk-in coolers.
What if my washer is making a loud noise?
Loud banging usually indicates bearing or suspension issues. Washer Wizards can often fix this in a single visit.
Is there a warranty on replacement parts?
Parts typically carry a 1-year manufacturer warranty, and technicians offer a separate workmanship guarantee.
Can a pro help install a new dryer?
Yes. Safe electrical and venting installation is a standard service provided by our appliance technicians.
How do I fix a dishwasher not draining?
Technicians can identify if it's a pump failure or a simple blockage in the drain hose during the first visit.
Do you fix broken TV panel screens?
TV specialists can replace panels for LED and OLED units if parts are available. Search for "Electronics" in the appliance section.

Personal Services Support

How are academic credentials verified?
Tutors are required to provide proof of degree or relevant certifications. We manually review these before granting the badge.
Are fitness sessions online or in-person?
Varies by pro. Individual profiles state if the expert travels to you, works at a studio, or offers virtual coaching.
Can I book a discovery or trial session?
Many tutors and language coaches offer a discounted first discovery session to ensure a good fit.
Do music instructors provide the instruments?
Usually not for home lessons. Studio-based instructors often provide large instruments like pianos or drum kits for use during the lesson.
How do I track student progress?
Verified tutors provide monthly session summaries highlighting areas of mastery and homework via the platform.
Are there discounts for lesson bundles?
Yes. Many trainers and tutors offer 5 or 10-session packages at a discounted rate compared to one-off bookings.
Can I hire a pro for a small group?
Yes. Yoga instructors and language tutors often host sessions for 2-3 people at a specific "Group Rate."
What is the cancellation policy?
Standard practice is a 24-hour notice for 1:1 sessions. Policies are clearly stated on the pro's "Booking" tab.
Is my child safe with a verified tutor?
Every pro who interacts with minors must pass an enhanced multi-state background check before profile activation.
Can I switch instructors if it's not a fit?
Absolutely. You are not locked into any provider. You can connect with any expert in our directory at any time.
Do photography pros provide gear?
Yes. Photography pros provide high-end cameras and lighting. You only need to discuss the creative brief.

General Platform Questions

Is Prime Local free for customers?
Yes. Neighbors pay nothing to search, view verified data, or contact professionals. We charge service pros to be listed.
Why do I need to register?
Registration ensures community safety and allows you to track communication history and save a shortlist of favorite pros.
How does the verification badge work?
The badge is awarded only after our team manually audits a business's legal license, insurance, and professional standing.
Is my personal data secure?
Yes. We use industry-standard encryption for all platform communication and never share your contact details until you initiate contact.
How are reviews moderated?
Every review is manually screened for authenticity. We cross-verify that a real interaction took place before publishing feedback.
What if a pro asks for off-platform payment?
Paying directly is standard. However, always request an invoice or receipt via the Prime Local portal for your records.
How do I report a safety concern?
There is a "Report" button on every profile. Our safety team investigates all flags within 12 business hours.
Can I book for multiple addresses?
Yes. You can manage several service locations (Home, Office, Rental) from your centralized user dashboard.
How do I reset my password?
Click "Forgot Password" on the login screen. A secure reset link will be sent to your registered email immediately.
What cities are covered?
We currently support major hubs like Houston, Chicago, and Hyderabad. More neighborhoods are added every month.
How do I delete my profile?
You can request account deletion via settings. All data is purged according to our strict privacy policy.
